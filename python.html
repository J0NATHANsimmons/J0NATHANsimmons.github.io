Tic-Tac-Toe

T T T

Three in a row!

Playing Tic-Tac-Toe is pretty simple, all you need to do is click a box, and try and get 3 of either X or O(depending on your team, and you win!

It is possible that you cannot win, and the game is tied.

Behind the scenes:

Here is the bulk of the game packed into one container, this controls the O and the X shown , and also alternates the turns
